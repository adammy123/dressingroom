The Problem

Sizes of clothes sold by online retailers tend to vary among different retailers

Different sizing charts do not take into account different body types

Sizing chart provided are too complicated to be used and read

Our Solution

Easy to Obtain measurement


Your measurements would be obtained by simply uploading 3 pictures of you in different angles. A custom 3D avatar would then be generated based on your profile.

Accurate Simulation


Our 3D avatar generated would be scaled according to your body measurements and virtual models of our clothings offered would be obtained using our 3D scanners, allowing you to accurately visualise how different clothes are going to look on you.

Hassle Free Shopping


Do your online shopping now without worrying about time delays or shipping fees incurred due to size misfits, by trying out your desired clothings virtually before making your purchase. Online shopping has never been easier.
